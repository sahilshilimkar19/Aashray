What is Aashray..?

Aashray!! as the name suggest home . Aashray is an compassionate website developed and designed to help homelesspeople to find them a shelter. In this app the user can click picture of a homeless person that he sees on streets and can post on our app/website the near by NGO will get the information of it and the location so their team can go their and do the needful. Additionally we provide a directory of additional resources, user safety and encourages feedback to continue enhance the user's experience . So let's join together to help us eradicate homelessness from INDIA and make a positive change in the society, which will make India from developing country to developed country....!

Why there is a need of Aashray..?

PUNE Pune has 5.29 per cent of its population below the poverty line after Mumbai at 3.59 per cent, according to ‘The state of India’s Environment 2022: In Figures,’ a report released by the Centre For Science and Environment (CSE). According to the report, Maharashtra stood at 19th position in the country with 14.87 per cent of its population under the Multidimensional Poverty Index (MPI) category. The data was shared under the section of Multidimensional Poverty Index in the world which looks at three dimensions of health, education and stand of living and twelve indicators comprising nutrition, school attendance, electricity, maternal health, sanitation, assets, child and adolescent mortality, cooking fuel, housing, years of schooling, drinking water and bank account. The need for “Aashray” arises from the pressing issue of homelessness and the need to provide effective support and shelter for individuals experiencing homelessness

Our Aim..!

Aashray is a website that aims to connect poor and homeless individuals with NGOs, addressing the challenges they face. The website provides a user-friendly interface for easy access to resources, empowering individuals to find suitable organizations for their specific needs, such as shelter, food and medical care. By facilitating connections between those in need and relevant organizations the website saves time and resources for both parties involved. It acts as a bridge, enabling efficient assistance delivery.
